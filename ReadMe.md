# Ketano Online Bookstore

**Ketano** is an online bookstore platform that allows users to **discover, explore, and purchase books** in a simple and enjoyable way. This application is designed to replicate the experience of a modern digital bookstore, providing a seamless and interactive environment for readers and book enthusiasts.

## Features

### For Readers

* **Browse and Search Books**: Easily find books by title, author, category, or rating.
* **Book Details**: View detailed information about each book, including description, author, price, and user ratings.
* **Shopping Experience**: Add books to your cart and place orders conveniently.
* **User Accounts**: Register, login, and manage personal profiles and order history.
* **Ratings and Reviews**: Share opinions and rate books to help other readers.

### For Administrators

* **Book Management**: Add, edit, or remove books from the catalog.
* **Category Management**: Organize books into meaningful categories for easier browsing.
* **Order Oversight**: View and manage orders placed by users.

## Purpose

The Ketano platform aims to **make books accessible to everyone** in a digital format, while providing a **clean, modern, and user-friendly experience**. It focuses on:

* Simplifying book discovery
* Enhancing online reading and shopping experiences
* Providing a platform for users to interact with books and authors

## User Experience

* **Intuitive Navigation**: Users can quickly browse and filter books.
* **Responsive Design**: Works well on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
* **Secure and Reliable**: User data and purchases are handled safely.
* **Interactive and Engaging**: Users can explore new releases, bestsellers, and curated recommendations.

## About the Project

Ketano is a **practice project** designed to **learn and demonstrate the functionalities of a modern online bookstore**. It focuses on **user experience, content management, and digital book shopping workflows**, while maintaining a **professional, clean, and trustworthy appearance**.

## Future Vision

* Expand book catalog with more categories and titles
* Introduce personalized recommendations for readers
* Enhance user interaction with reviews, ratings, and wishlists
* Integrate digital content like e-books and audiobooks
